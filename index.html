Hi there - let’s cross heritage spaces! Kaan (a binaural microphone by sound.codes) is currently “listening” to the old Bärenzwinger of Berlin, transplanting the acoustics of this former bear pit into ancient heritage spaces of Goan history in order to let you experience India's past. This is possible through the unique acoustic data that endured through time and has been collected and stored in form of “acoustic signatures” (or acoustic reverb values). Because every space has its unique acoustic properties, due to the combination of its dimensions, architectural design and the use of construction materials - with our ears we can stroke the boundaries of these spaces and go on a journey through time…

Navigate through time and space by clicking through the images - transplanting the acoustics of the bear pit.
